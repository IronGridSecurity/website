The Importance of Log Aggregation

Log aggregation enables faster detection, incident response, and compliance. We explore how tools like Splunk, ELK, and Graylog centralize logs for analysis, and what SMBs can do with minimal budgets.

Use Cases

  • Detect lateral movement
  • Trace insider threats
  • Prove compliance during audits

Proper log retention and parsing are essential. Without context, logs are just noise.
