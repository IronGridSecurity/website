Anatomy of a Phishing Attack

Phishing continues to be one of the most effective attack vectors. In this article, we dissect a real-world phishing attempt from subject line to payload delivery, explaining how the attacker bypassed defenses and what security teams can do to stop them.

Indicators of Compromise (IOCs)

  • Lookalike domain: irongr1dsecurity.com
  • Obfuscated link redirection
  • Fake login page built with Bootstrap

Lessons Learned

Train users to hover over links, report suspicious emails, and validate domain authenticity. Implement DMARC and external email warnings where possible.
